OSCAR

Organização Socio-Cooperativa de AstRofísica

The Astronomy and Astrophysics Master is divided into two years. There are four obligatory courses in the first semester and four obligatory ones in the second semester of the first year. The second year is mostly thesis. This page was made by students for students. Here you will find exercises, exams, and (hopefully) solutions from previous years. If you'd like to add anything to the bank, please contact us.
