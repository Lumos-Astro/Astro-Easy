Bringing the light to your study.

The Astronomy and Astrophysics Master is divided in two years. There are three obligatory courses in the first semester and five obligatory ones in the second semester of the first year. The second year is mostly thesis. This page was made from students for students. Here you will find exercises, exams and (hopefully) solutions from previous years. If you'd like to add anything to the bank please contact us.

First Semester:

  • Estrutura e Evolução Estelar
  • Sistemas Planetários
  • Fotometria e Espetometria

Second Semester:

  • Análise de Dados em Física e Astronomia
  • Astronomia Extragalática
  • Cosmologia
  • Formação Estelar e Meio Circum-Estelar
  • O Sol e a Heliosfera

Opcionais:

  • Relatividade Geral
  • Mecânica Quântica Avançada
  • Astrobiologia
  • Telescópios e Detetores para as Ciências do Espaço
