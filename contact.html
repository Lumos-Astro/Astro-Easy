Contacts

Bringing the light to your study

If you need help, there are many people you can talk to. For course-related issues like exam dates or homework extensions, your professors should be the first point of contact.

The Comissão de Acompanhamento de Curso consists of four people: the main professor (head of the Master), another professor (usually head of the Doctorate program), and two students. Meetings take place at the end of each semester, but you don’t need to wait for the semester's end to raise issues.

You can also explore internship opportunities with professors and research centers like the Centro de Investigação da Universidade do Porto (CAUP) and Instituto de Astrofísica e Ciências do Espaço (IA).
